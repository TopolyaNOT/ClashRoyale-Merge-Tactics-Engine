

## Stats

### level:
Unit level that makes impact on base stats

### star:
Key unit improvment
can be 1, 2, 3, 4 
1 - min, 4 - max

### elixir
Base elixer cost: 

- 2 - Knight, Archers, Goblins, Barbarians, Skeleton Dragons, Wizard, Dart Goblin
- 3 - Giant, Musketeer, Valkurie, Royal Giant, Giant Skeleton, Goblin Demolisher, Royal Ghost, Executioner
- 4 - P.E.K.K.A, Witch, Baby Gragon, Prince, Princess, Bandit, Goblin Machine
- 5 - Skeleton King, Golden Knight, Archer Queen, Monk

### hitpoints
HP that depends on stars and level

### damage
DM that depends on stars and level

### speed:
- 0 - very slow
- 1 - slow
- 2 - medium
- 3 - fast
- 4 - very fast

### hit speed
The time interval (in seconds) between a troop's attacks.

- Lower value: The unit attacks faster (e.g., Dart Goblin).
- Higher value: The unit attacks slower but usually hits harder (e.g., P.E.K.K.A).

### range
The distance from which a unit can target and attack an enemy.

- Melee: Must be right next to the target (Short, Medium, or Long melee range).
- Ranged: Can hit from a distance (e.g., Musketeer or Princess).


### criticak_chance
The probability (percentage) that an attack will deal extra damage.
